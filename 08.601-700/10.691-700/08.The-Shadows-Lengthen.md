#### Advent Hymnals
## 698. THE SHADOWS LENGTHEN
####  Church Hymnal 1941,

```txt
1.
The shadows lengthen, Lord, with me abide. 
O Son of God, 
Be Thou my Friend and Guide; 
Through all the coming days 
Whate'er betide' 
In the cleft Rock of Ages 
Let me hide 
In peace, perfect peace!
```
